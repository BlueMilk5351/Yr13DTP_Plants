About Plants

General information

Plants are living organisms that use sunlight and water to produce energy through photosynthesis. They also produce oxygen, which is released into the air for us to breathe. Plants are made up of stems, leaves and roots. The stems support the plant so that the leaves can absorb sunlight, along with the roots that absorb water.
